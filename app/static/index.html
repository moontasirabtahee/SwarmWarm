How SwarmWarm operates

Decentralised email deliverability repair in three clear steps.

01

Secure node onboarding

Connect standard SMTP/IMAP credentials. The app encrypts them with AES-256-GCM before writing anything to storage.

02

P2P pairing consensus

The scheduler builds daily non-looping transaction paths across different domains to simulate realistic correspondence.

03

Contextual AI threading

Workers dispatch sends, rescue messages from spam, and reply contextually via local Gemma inference.
