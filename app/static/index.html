Email Warmup Swarm

Automate SMTP & IMAP inbox warmup exchanges across isolated sender fleets. Utilizes localized Gemma LLM models to simulate natural B2B interactions and recover sender domain reputation organically.

P2P Warmup Network

Automatically match mailboxes inside the swarm using a bipartite directed graph scheduling algorithm to maximize cross-provider coverage.

Local Gemma 4B AI

Simulates contextual replies, automatic RFC threading headers (In-Reply-To), and natural language topics over reverse-proxy tunnels.

Symmetric GCM Encryption

Your SMTP and IMAP passwords are never saved in plaintext. AES-256-GCM symmetric byte encryption secures all credentials locally.

How SwarmWarm Operates

Decentralized email deliverability repair in three clear steps

01

Secure Node Onboarding

Connect standard SMTP/IMAP credentials. The app immediately encodes it using client-side GCM encryption before transit.

02

P2P Pairing Consensus

Our matching algorithm builds daily non-looping transaction paths across different domains to simulate realistic correspondence.

03

Contextual AI Threading

Celery queues execute sends and scan spam folders to move inbox messages back, replying contextually via local Gemma inference.
